Module 1: Mouse Skills

The Magic of the Mouse 🖱️

Left Click: The "Do It" button. Use this to select items and open apps.
Right Click: The "Secret Menu." It shows you extra options you can't see!
Scroll Wheel: The "Elevator." Use it to move up and down long pages.
Drag & Drop: The "Grabber." Pick up an item and move it to a new home.

Core Lesson

The Digital Post Office 🌐

Imagine the Internet is a Giant World Map. Every computer, phone, and tablet is a "house" on that map.

To visit a house, you need an Address (URL). To send a letter, you use Email. Instead of paper, your message is turned into digital "packets" that fly through wires under the ocean to reach your friends!

The Ant and the Cricket

Reading Time: 4 mins • Theme: Discipline

During the golden months of the dry season, the savannah was full of life and music.

The Cricket spent his days singing and dancing under the warm sun. Whenever he saw the Ants passing by, struggling to carry heavy grains of maize back to their nest, he would laugh. "Why do you work so hard?" he chirped. "The sun is shining and there is plenty of food. Come, dance with me!"

"The best time to plant a tree was twenty years ago. The second best time is now."

The Lead Ant paused to wipe his brow. "The rains will come soon, Cricket. If we do not gather food now, we will be hungry later." But the Cricket only laughed louder and continued his song.

When the heavy clouds finally broke and the Great Rains flooded the plains, the Cricket could find no food. Shivering and hungry, he went to the Ants' nest. The Ants were warm and well-fed. They shared their food with him, but the Cricket realized that while singing is beautiful, it cannot fill an empty stomach during the rain.

🐜 The Tech Connection

Learning computer skills is exactly like the Ants' work. You might want to play games all day (like the Cricket), but practicing your typing and studying code today is how you "gather food" for your future career. Hard work today leads to an easy life tomorrow!

Future-Self Check:

If you practice your computer lessons for 20 minutes every day, what happens after one month?

Privacy Policy — Effective: April 2026

Privacy &
Security Policy

At Kidventor Academy, we value the trust of students, parents, teachers, and schools. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and manage your information across our educational platform.

01. Our Commitment

Kidventor Academy is an educational platform focused on coding classes for kids, keyboard and mouse training, digital literacy, CBT preparation, and moral education. We are committed to protecting the privacy of children, parents, schools, and all visitors using our website.

02. Information We Collect

We may collect limited information necessary to provide a better learning experience:

Personal Details

Name, email address, and school-related details submitted through forms.

Learning Progress

Worksheet activity, quiz progress, and learning preferences stored locally.

Technical Data

Browser type, device details, and basic usage information for improvement.

03. How We Use Information

  • • To improve student learning experience and lesson delivery
  • • To respond to inquiries from parents, schools, and students
  • • To support worksheets, quizzes, and CBT preparation tools
  • • To improve website performance, safety, and educational quality

04. Data Protection Promise

We do not sell, rent, or trade student or parent information. Ever.

We collect only the minimum information needed for educational support and communication. Protecting children and families is one of our highest priorities.

05. Cookies & Google AdSense

Kidventor Academy may use cookies to improve your browsing experience and educational services. We also use Google AdSense to display advertisements.

Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your previous visits to this and other websites. Users may manage ad preferences through Google Ad Settings.

06. Local Storage & Security

Some student progress features use localStorage within your browser to save progress and improve user experience. This helps students continue learning without unnecessary repeated steps.

07. Contact for Privacy Questions

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, data removal, or information security, please contact us directly.

kidventor2026@gmail.com

08. Your Rights

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of submitted personal information where applicable. You may also clear browser storage to remove saved local progress data.

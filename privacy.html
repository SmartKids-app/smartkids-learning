Privacy &
Security Protocol

At Kidventor Academy, we protect your data like it's the core source code of our future. Here is how we manage your information.

01. Mission Statement

Kidventor is an educational environment. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our students, parents, and mentors. This protocol governs our data handling across all educational tools, typing modules, and coding simulators.

02. Data Acquisition

To maintain high-performance learning, we record the following parameters:

👤

Identity

Name, age, grade level, and school affiliation.

🔑

Account

Username, secure school email, and region.

📊

Metrics

Typing WPM, quiz accuracy, and code task completion.

03. Deployment

Your data is used strictly for technical and educational advancement:

  • Tracking progress across the curriculum
  • Generating progress reports for parents/mentors
  • Debugging and improving the platform interface

04. The Guardian Clause (COPPA)

We never sell, rent, or trade student data. Period.

We follow COPPA standards to the letter, collecting only the absolute minimum amount of information required for your educational growth.

🛡️

05. Cookies & Tracking

Kidventor uses Google AdSense for platform sustainability. Google uses cookies to serve non-intrusive ads based on your site visits. You can manage your preferences or opt-out at any time via Google Ad Settings.

06. Encryption & Storage

We utilize IndexedDB for secure, client-side offline storage. All data transmissions are encrypted using industry-standard protocols to prevent unauthorized interception.

07. Support

Have a question about your data or security? Contact our Data Guardian:

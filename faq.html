FAQ — Common Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

Here are answers to the most common questions from parents, schools, and students about Kidventor Academy, our coding lessons, worksheets, digital literacy training, and learning support.

1. What is Kidventor Academy?

Kidventor Academy is a learning platform that teaches children coding, keyboard and mouse skills, digital literacy, CBT preparation, problem-solving, and moral values. Our goal is to help children become creators of technology, not just users.

2. What age group do you teach?

We support children from beginner learners to teenagers. Lessons are structured to match different levels—from early keyboard and mouse practice to advanced website building and coding logic.

3. Do students need prior computer knowledge?

No. Many students begin with zero experience. We start from the basics, including how to use a mouse, typing practice, safe internet habits, and computer confidence.

4. What courses do you offer?

Our courses include keyboard skills, mouse control, digital literacy, CBT exam preparation, coding basics, website development, algorithm thinking, worksheets, and character education through moral lessons.

5. Is Kidventor Academy for schools too?

Yes. We work with schools, school owners, and educational centers to support ICT curriculum development, student training, and digital learning programs.

6. Are your worksheets free?

Some worksheets and learning resources are freely available, while others may be part of structured learning sessions or school programs.

7. How do I contact your team?

You can contact us through our Contact page, email, or WhatsApp for enrollment, school partnerships, parent support, or general inquiries.

kidventor2026@gmail.com

8. Do you prepare students for CBT exams?

Yes. We help students practice typing speed, mouse accuracy, computer confidence, and exam navigation skills needed for CBT-based tests and school assessments.

9. Is the platform safe for children?

Yes. Child safety, privacy, and responsible learning are central to our mission. We focus on safe educational experiences and responsible digital habits.

10. Can parents request custom support?

Absolutely. Parents can reach out for guidance on student progress, learning plans, digital literacy support, and personalized educational recommendations.

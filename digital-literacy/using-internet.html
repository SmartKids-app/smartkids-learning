Using the Internet 🌐

The internet helps us search for information, attend online classes, send emails, watch lessons, and communicate with people around the world. It is like a giant digital library connecting the whole world together.

What is the Internet?

The internet is a worldwide network of computers connected together. It helps people share information, learn new things, send messages, and work from anywhere in the world.

How We Use the Internet 💡

Online Classes

Students can attend classes and watch lessons from home.

Research

We can search for school assignments and project information.

Email & Chat

We send messages and communicate with teachers and friends.

Entertainment

We watch videos, play games, and listen to music safely.

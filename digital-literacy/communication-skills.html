The Power of Words

Whether you are sending an email, chatting in a game, or talking on Zoom, how you speak tells the world who you are. Let's become communication pros!

"HEY GIVE ME THE FILE NOW!!!"
⬇️ THE POLITENESS FILTER ⬇️
"Hi there! Could you please send me the file when you have a moment? Thank you! 😊"

Texting vs. Talking

When you type, people cannot see your face or hear your voice. This means small things like CAPITAL LETTERS can make you sound like you are shouting!

  • ✅ Use "Please" and "Thank You"
  • ✅ Use emojis to show your mood
  • ❌ Avoid writing in ALL CAPS

How to Write a Pro Email 📧

Subject Line

Keep it short (e.g., "Question about Lesson 2")

Greeting

Start with "Dear Teacher" or "Hello Mr. Smith"

The Message

Be clear and explain exactly what you need help with.

Closing

End with "Best regards" or "Sincerely" and your name.

🔇

Mute Yourself

Stay on mute if you aren't talking so others don't hear background noise.

👀

Eye Contact

Look at the camera, not just the screen, so people feel you are looking at them.

✋

Wait Your Turn

Use the "Raise Hand" button instead of interrupting the teacher.

The 3-Second Rule

"Before you hit 'Send' or 'Post', wait 3 seconds. Ask yourself: Is it Kind? Is it True? Is it Necessary?"

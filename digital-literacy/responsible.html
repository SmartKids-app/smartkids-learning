The Wise Digital Citizen

With great tech comes great responsibility. Every click you make and every word you type leaves a mark. Let's make sure your mark is a great one!

Your Digital Footprint 👣

Everything you do online—comments, photos, and searches—is like a permanent footprint in the sand that never washes away.

"The internet never forgets. Before you post, ask: Would I want my future boss or my grandmother to see this?"

Be an "Upstander," Not a Bystander 🛡️

If you see someone being mean online, you have the power to stop it.

Don't Feed the Trolls

Mean people want a reaction. If someone is being rude, don't argue back. Block them and move on.

Report & Support

Tell a trusted adult and check on the person who was being bullied. A kind message can save someone's day.
